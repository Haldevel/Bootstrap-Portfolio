what2grow
See the project
See the code

This is a web application designed to help amateur gardeners make informed decisions about the plants they choose to grow. With help of autocomplete, the user types in a crop or plant of their choice. The app will provide the first suitable month and the optimal month for the plant to grow based on the user's geolocation, as well on the historical daily min, max, and mean temperatures for 2015-2018 years, received from aWhere API. The web app is a product of collaboration between four people.
